Optimize Swiss accounting with our Odoo balance account module.

Although Odoo already provides a strong foundation for Swiss accounting, it previously lacked the ability to specify a balance account for each individual posting line. Our custom-developed module addresses this limitation, ensuring compliance with the legal requirements of double-entry bookkeeping while also enhancing transparency and traceability for trustees, auditors, and financial professionals.

Seamless integration of balance accounts into every booking line.

Our module enhances Odoo by adding the crucial functionality of displaying the balance account for each posting line in the general ledger. The relevant balance account is automatically identified and recorded for every transaction, ensuring your financial data remains accurate, reliable, and audit-ready.


Generate clear, audit-proof financial reports with just a single click.

Leverage our app to generate comprehensive and accurate general ledgers directly within Odoo. With each posting line featuring the corresponding balance account, you gain insightful, transparent, and audit-proof reports that fully comply with Swiss accounting standards.
